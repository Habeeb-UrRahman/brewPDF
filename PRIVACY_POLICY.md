# Privacy Policy for brewPDF

**Last updated: July 1, 2026**

Brew Creative Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the brewPDF Android application.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
brewPDF is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or document contents. 

## 2. Local Processing
All PDF processing (merging, splitting, encryption, etc.) occurs locally on your device. Your documents never leave your phone, and we do not use any cloud servers or third-party analytics.

## 3. Permissions
brewPDF requires no Android permissions (such as Internet, Storage, or Camera). It uses secure, standard Android system pickers to access the files you explicitly choose to edit.

## 4. Open Source
brewPDF is open source software distributed under the GNU AGPL v3 license. Our source code is publicly available for auditing to verify these privacy claims.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
